Why spend $1,000 on a gaming PC when you can spend $100,000 on a Tesla instead?

Elon Musk’s electric car company released a holiday software update on Tuesday and with it came the ability for recent Model S and Model X vehicles (specifically, from the last two years) to play Steam games. Yes, you read that right: Steam, the world’s foremost PC gaming storefront, is now available in car form, per Electrek.
